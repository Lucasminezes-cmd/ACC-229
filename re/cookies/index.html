Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Stallion Health Journal uses them, and how you can control them. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work properly, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owner about how the site is used.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors interact with the site (for example, which pages are visited most). These may include tools such as Google Analytics.
  • Advertising cookies: may be set through our site by advertising partners to help measure and improve the relevance of ads you see.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties, such as analytics and advertising providers. These parties process data under their own privacy and cookie policies, over which we have no control.

Managing & Disabling Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being set. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect how the website functions. For guidance, consult the "Help" section of your browser.

Your Consent

By continuing to use this website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw or change your preferences at any time through your browser settings.
